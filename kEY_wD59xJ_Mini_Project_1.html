Harry Potter


Introduction:

Harry Potter is a famous book written by J.K Rowling. More than 600 million books were sold. There are 7 books and 8 movies in total. The last book is split into 2 parts.

Who Is Harry Potter:

Harry Potter was born from a wizard family, but then Voldy-Moldy-Voldemort(THE BAD GUY) killed his parents when he was one years old. Harry was sent to live with his aunt and uncle, and didn't realize he was a wizard until later. Harry went to Hogwarts, a magical school that teaches witches and wizards.

Accomplishments:

Harry, along with Hermione and Ron, help save the wizarding world from Voldemort. Harry faces lots of near-death situations and escapes from them. Can he save the world from Voldemort? Or is it not possible? Read the series or watch the movies to find out!
